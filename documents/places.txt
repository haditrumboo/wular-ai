FAMOUS PLACES IN KASHMIR:

Dal Lake - Srinagar
Dal Lake is one of the most famous lakes in Kashmir located in Srinagar.
Famous for Shikara rides, houseboats, and floating vegetable markets.
Best time to visit: April to October.
Distance from Srinagar airport: 15km.

Gulmarg
Gulmarg is a famous hill station located 56km from Srinagar.
Famous for skiing in winter and green meadows in summer.
Has the highest gondola cable car in the world.
Best time to visit: December to February for snow, May to September for meadows.

Pahalgam
Pahalgam is located 95km from Srinagar.
Famous for Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari.
Starting point for Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage.
Best time to visit: April to October.

Sonamarg
Sonamarg means Meadow of Gold.
Located 80km from Srinagar.
Famous for Thajiwas Glacier and Zero Point.
Best time to visit: May to September.

Srinagar
Capital of Kashmir. Famous for Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake, and old city bazaars.
Must visit: Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi, Hazratbal Shrine, Jamia Masjid.

HIDDEN PLACES IN KASHMIR:

Bangus Valley
Bangus Valley is located near Kupwara, 150km from Srinagar.
One of the most hidden and unexplored valleys in Kashmir.
Very few tourists visit this place.
Famous for dense forests, alpine meadows, and wildlife.
Best time to visit: June to September.

Yusmarg
Yusmarg is a hidden meadow located 47km from Srinagar.
Much less crowded than Gulmarg but equally beautiful.
Famous for Doodh Ganga river and pine forests.
Best time to visit: April to September.

Lolab Valley
Lolab Valley is located near Kupwara.
Known as the paradise of Kashmir but very few tourists know about it.
Famous for apple orchards, trout streams, and dense forests.
Best time to visit: April to October.

Tulail Valley
Tulail Valley is located in Gurez district, 173km from Srinagar.
One of the most remote and beautiful valleys in Kashmir.
Famous for Kishanganga river and Dard tribe culture.
Best time to visit: June to September.

Sinthan Top
Sinthan Top is a high altitude mountain pass at 3800 meters.
Located between Kashmir and Kishtwar.
Hidden gem for adventure tourists.
Famous for snow covered roads and panoramic views.
Best time to visit: June to August.

Wular Lake
Wular Lake is one of the largest freshwater lakes in Asia.
Located 60km from Srinagar near Sopore.
Less visited but very scenic.
Best time to visit: March to October.

Doodhpathri
Doodhpathri means Bowl of Milk.
Located 42km from Srinagar.
Famous for white foamy streams running through green meadows.
Very less crowded and peaceful.
Best time to visit: May to September.
