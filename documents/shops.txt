KASHMIRI ART SHOPS AND CRAFTS:

Pashmina Shawls
Pashmina shawls are the most famous product of Kashmir.
Made from Changthangi goat wool from Ladakh.
A real Pashmina shawl takes 3 to 6 months to make by hand.
Where to buy: Polo View Market Srinagar, Lal Chowk, Residency Road.
Price range: Genuine Pashmina starts from 3000 rupees to 50000 rupees.
Tip: Buy from government certified shops to avoid fake Pashmina.

Kashmiri Carpets
Hand knotted woolen carpets are a major craft of Kashmir.
Each carpet takes months or years to complete.
Famous for intricate floral and geometric patterns.
Where to buy: Carpet shops in Srinagar, Shah-e-Hamdan area.
Price range: Small carpets from 5000 rupees, large carpets can cost lakhs.

Walnut Wood Crafts
Kashmir is famous for walnut wood furniture and decorative items.
Products include bowls, trays, boxes, furniture, wall hangings.
Where to buy: Craft shops in Srinagar, Ram Munshi Bagh area.
Price range: Small items from 500 rupees.

Papier Mache
Papier Mache is a traditional Kashmiri craft.
Items include boxes, vases, bowls, Christmas ornaments.
Painted with intricate designs of flowers, birds, and geometric patterns.
Where to buy: Craft bazaars in Srinagar old city.
Price range: Small items from 200 rupees.

Kashmiri Saffron
Kashmir produces the best saffron in the world.
Grown in Pampore, 15km from Srinagar.
Used in cooking, tea, and medicine.
Where to buy: Pampore saffron fields, Lal Chowk shops.
Price range: 300 to 500 rupees per gram for genuine saffron.

Kani Shawls
Kani shawls are woven using special wooden sticks called Kanis.
Each shawl takes 1 to 2 years to complete.
Have geographical indication tag from Indian government.
Where to buy: Kanihama village near Srinagar, craft emporiums.

Sozni Embroidery
Sozni is a fine needle embroidery done on Pashmina and silk.
Used on shawls, suits, and sarees.
Where to buy: Old city shops in Srinagar.

Government Emporiums
J&K Government Arts Emporium - Polo View Srinagar.
Sells certified authentic Kashmiri handicrafts.
Best place for tourists to buy genuine products without being cheated.
