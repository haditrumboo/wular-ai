7 DAY KASHMIR TRIP PLAN:

Day 1 - Arrival in Srinagar
Arrive at Srinagar airport.
Check into houseboat on Dal Lake.
Evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake.
Try Wazwan dinner at a local restaurant.
Stay: Houseboat on Dal Lake.

Day 2 - Srinagar City Tour
Visit Mughal Gardens: Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi.
Visit Hazratbal Shrine.
Visit Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic view of Srinagar.
Evening walk in Lal Chowk bazaar.
Shopping for Kashmiri saffron and dry fruits.
Stay: Srinagar.

Day 3 - Gulmarg Day Trip
Drive to Gulmarg (56km, 2 hours).
Take Gondola cable car ride to Kongdori and Apharwat Peak.
Snow activities in winter season.
Meadow walk in summer season.
Return to Srinagar in evening.
Stay: Srinagar.

Day 4 - Pahalgam
Drive to Pahalgam (95km, 3 hours).
Visit Betaab Valley.
Visit Aru Valley.
Horse riding to Chandanwari.
Stay: Pahalgam.

Day 5 - Sonamarg
Drive to Sonamarg (87km from Pahalgam).
Visit Thajiwas Glacier by pony ride or walk.
Visit Zero Point if snow is accessible.
Drive back to Srinagar.
Stay: Srinagar.

Day 6 - Hidden Gems Day
Visit Yusmarg meadow (47km from Srinagar).
Walk along Doodh Ganga river.
Visit Doodhpathri if time allows.
Evening shopping for Pashmina shawls and Walnut wood crafts in Srinagar.
Stay: Srinagar.

Day 7 - Departure
Morning Shikara ride on Dal Lake at sunrise.
Visit old city and Jamia Masjid.
Last minute shopping at Polo View Market.
Depart from Srinagar airport.
