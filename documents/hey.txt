

# 10 FAQs for better retrieval compatibility

Q1: What are the best business ideas in Kashmir?
A: Top opportunities include tourism/hospitality, handicrafts (Pashmina, carpets,
   walnut wood), saffron farming, fruit processing, IT services, and agri-tourism.

Q2: Which sectors are most profitable for startups in Kashmir?
A: Tourism, horticulture (apple/saffron exports), handloom, and digital services
   consistently rank among the highest-return sectors.

Q3: How can I start a handicraft business in Kashmir?
A: Register with the J&K Handicrafts Department, source artisans locally, obtain a
   GI-tag certification if applicable, and list on e-commerce platforms like Amazon
   Karigar or IndiaMART.

Q4: What government schemes support businesses in Kashmir?
A: Key schemes include the J&K Industrial Policy 2021-30, PM Employment Generation
   Programme (PMEGP), and MUDRA loans under Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana.

Q5: Is tourism a good business in Kashmir?
A: Yes. With 2+ crore annual visitors, houseboat rentals, adventure tourism (skiing,
   trekking), and eco-resorts offer strong returns, especially in Gulmarg and Pahalgam.

Q6: What is the investment needed to start a small business in Kashmir?
A: Small-scale ventures (crafts, food stalls, homestays) can start with ₹50,000–₹5 lakh.
   Medium enterprises like hotels or processing units need ₹10–₹50 lakh+.

Q7: Are there any unique agricultural businesses in Kashmir?
A: Saffron cultivation (Pampore), walnut orchards, cherry and apple farming, and
   lavender cultivation are highly unique and export-ready agri-businesses.

Q8: How does the internet/digital economy apply to Kashmir businesses?
A: E-commerce for handicrafts, online travel agencies, digital marketing for local
   tourism, and remote IT/freelancing are growing rapidly post-4G/5G expansion.

Q9: What challenges do businesses face in Kashmir?
A: Seasonal disruptions, supply chain issues in winter, connectivity constraints in
   remote areas, and limited access to formal credit are common challenges.

Q10: Where can I get funding for a new business in Kashmir?
A: Sources include J&K Bank MSME loans, government grants via JKEDI (J&K
   Entrepreneurship Development Institute), angel networks, and SIDBI schemes.
